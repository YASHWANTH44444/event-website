ABOUT

Since 1984, B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology is a renowned Quality
Leadership Institution located at the greenest spot of Chennai near Tambaram. Through our long
history of 38 years of excellence, the Institution has offered access to a wide range of academic
opportunities. With 55 programmes, grouped under 12 different Schools, 30 Undergraduate
programmes, 25 Postgraduate programmes, and Ph.D. (in all the departments), this institution is a
rising stalwart in higher education with promising Quality, Security and Placement. We welcome
students from all countries and our educational programmes are designed to equip the learners
with virtual knowledge that helps them to achieve what they want to be and go where they want
to go in the ladder of success.
